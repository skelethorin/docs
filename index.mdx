## Who We Are

Founded by industry veterans who have repeatedly collaborated across top-tier blockchain projects, Krews was born out of a shared vision: to provide the most robust, transparent, and sustainable validator services in the ecosystem. We bring together deep technical expertise, best-in-class infrastructure, and an unwavering commitment to your success.

## Our Mission

- **Security First**: We leverage enterprise-grade servers equipped with ECC memory, redundant power supplies, and global sentry nodes to guard against downtime and slashes.
- **Decentralization Advocates**: By spreading our operations across multiple continents and providers, we harden the networks we serve—making them stronger for everyone.
- **Sustainable Growth**: Krews embraces eco-friendly practices and energy-efficient hardware to minimize our carbon footprint without sacrificing performance.

## What We Do

- **24/7 Live Monitoring**\
  Continuous, automated alerting combined with an expert on-call team ensures that any anomaly is caught and addressed immediately.
- **Bare-Metal Infrastructure**\
  We deploy dedicated bare-metal servers in premier data centers worldwide—never virtualized, never shared—ensuring maximum performance, predictability, and control.
- **Comprehensive Protection**\
  Our multi-layer defense strategy guards your stake against minor slashes, and our rapid-response protocols maximize uptime and compensation in the rare event of network penalties.

## Why Choose Krews

1. **Proven Expertise**\
   Years of hands-on experience running validators across Avail, Story, Celestia, EigenLayer, Lava Network, and more.
2. **Enterprise-Grade Reliability**\
   SLAs backed by real-time monitoring, failover sentries, and on-demand support—so you never miss a block.
3. **Transparent Operations**\
   Clear reporting, open metrics, and full access to our Explorer and Snapshot services let you see exactly what we’re doing, minute-by-minute.
