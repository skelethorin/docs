## **What is the Krews?**

Krews was founded by a group of professionals with many years of experience in the industry. We have come together in the past in different projects and combined their knowledge and experience in the blockchain field to create this structure. Our goal is to use this knowledge to provide the best validator service to projects communities and our delegators. We are here to empower your projects, fulfill various needs and contribute to your sustainable success in the blockchain space.
